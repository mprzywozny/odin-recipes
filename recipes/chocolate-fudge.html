Chocolate Fudge

Description

These rich, creamy, and delicious fudge treats are perfect for special occasions or holiday dessert tables.

Ingredients:

  • 150ml sunflower oil, plus extra for the tin
  • 175g self-raising flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 150g caster sugar
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 150ml semi-skimmed milk
